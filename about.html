PhotographyFirst Love

Over the past three years, I have been passionately immersed in the captivating world of photography, and I take great pride in my skills behind the lens. My journey began with capturing moments during college events, where I discovered the joy of freezing memories in time. Whether it's documenting the excitement of cultural festivals, the camaraderie of sports events, or the essence of academic gatherings, I have consistently strived to capture the essence of each moment with creativity and precision. Additionally, photography has become an integral part of my personal life, bringing me immense pleasure and fulfillment. Exploring diverse landscapes, candid portraits, and even the subtle beauty of everyday life has been an enriching experience. Through my lens, I strive to convey emotions, evoke nostalgia, and tell stories that leave a lasting impression on those who view my work. Photography is not just a hobby for me; it is a passion that continues to drive and inspire me each day.
